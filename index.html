An Evening with SC&P

Dear Friends and Clients,

The time comes, yet again, to celebrate the end of a decade -- and what a turbulent decade it's been. In a mere ten years, we've witnessed the continuation of a tragic, polarising war, the assasination of political figures, the death of icons, and the rise of civil rights and sexual liberation. And while it's tempting to look back on this decade cynically, it's also important to look to the future with hope, in that good things will come of what's ahead.

Regardless, the occasion calls for a tropical reprieve: Sydney, Australia.

While Sydney may not have the snowdrifts of New York, don’t let the sunshine fool you, as nights by the water can grow nippy. The occasion calls for suits and ties, or fashionable clubwear: polos, sports jackets, cardigans.

For the ladies: as Abraham Lincoln once put it, "the higher the hair, the better the **luck** -- but the shorter the **dress**, the better the --" (he was cut-off mid-sentence by a waiter dropping a tray).

Beatniks and hippies welcome, provided they bring the "good vibes" with them and don't arrive shoeless.

We require your response by December 7.

-- R. STERLING
